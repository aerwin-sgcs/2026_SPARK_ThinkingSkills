Discussion & Reflection

Reflective questions to think with after — or alongside — a Stretch Task. Your responses save automatically to this browser; nothing is shared.

← Back to Stretch Tasks

The questions below ask you to think about how you learned, not just what you produced. They draw on the same Learnership ideas that the Stretch Tasks engage — the Learning Zone, effective effort, and what mistakes make possible. Pick the section that matches your most recent task, or work through all five.

Inquiry & Investigation

Question 1

When did I step into my Learning Zone by exploring a question or idea that was unfamiliar to me?

Question 2

What mistake or unexpected result helped me learn something new during my investigation?

Problem Solving & Design

Question 1

How did I show effective effort when my first solution or design did not work?

Question 2

What did I learn from a mistake that helped improve my final solution or design?

Language & Communication

Question 1

When did I challenge myself to communicate an idea in a new or more effective way?

Question 2

How did I respond when I misunderstood something or made a communication mistake?

Ethics & Philosophy

Question 1

What idea or perspective pushed me into my Learning Zone by making me rethink my assumptions?

Question 2

How did I use effective effort to understand a viewpoint that was different from my own?

Creative & Imaginative

Question 1

When did I take a creative risk that stretched my thinking beyond my Comfort Zone?

Question 2

How did a mistake or failed idea contribute to a stronger creative outcome?
